# SOMA Hero Loop — 7 Pillars Prompt

For re-rolling variants in `/ai-image` or Krea.

## 7 Pillars

**1. Subject**
A single hand at rest on a fold of raw natural linen, slowly rising and falling with breath. Anonymous — framing ends at the wrist. A sprig of rosemary with tiny lime-green leaves at the edge of frame in soft focus.

**2. Environment**
Quiet morning interior. Raw oak wood surface or stoneware ceramic tray. Background blurred: a terracotta pot with a green plant, folded throw blanket in cream linen. Studio-loft / garden-room aesthetic.

**3. Lighting**
Soft directional daylight from frame-left through a sheer linen curtain. Slightly hazy. Golden-hour edge spill on the linen fold. Low contrast. No hard shadows. No flicker.

**4. Camera**
Locked-off macro shot. 50mm prime feel. Shallow depth of field (f/1.8). No camera movement — only the subject breathes. Cinematic 24fps with subtle natural motion blur.

**5. Style**
Editorial wellness cinematography in the register of Aesop, Le Labo, Apple Health campaigns. Anti-stock. Slow, intentional, contemplative. No music cues. No lifestyle cliches.

**6. Palette**
Forest green `#363D33` · off-white cream `#F4F5F0` · warm sand `#F5E0BF` · sunset peach `#FCC57F` · pale lavender-blue `#B6C8FB`. Lime green `#A4DA73` only as a living botanical accent — never neon.

**7. Mood**
Exhale. Grounded. The first slow breath after a long day. The footage should make a busy person feel calmer within three seconds.

## Loop constraint

6-second seamless loop. Breath pattern (~4s exhale + 2s inhale) ends exactly where it begins. Subject choice (breathing hand on linen) supports natural looping.
