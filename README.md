### Editorial systeem

Twee design-systemen leven naast elkaar:

**LAH brand mockup system** — Montserrat, DLS-palette (navy `#12345A` / cream `#FFF8E7` / lime `#DEFEB3`), tonale gradiënten, atmosferische illustraties uit de LAH brand library. Gebruikt op de meeste slides.

**Lilibeth DNA system** — geëxtraheerd uit het *Your Moodboard*-deck van Lilibeth Bustos. Warm off-white paper `#F0F0EE`, near-black ink, single acid-lime accent `#D6FF5C`, hairline rules, light-weight display type, genereuze negative space. Toegepast op slide 10 (Colors) als proof-of-concept.

Volledige analyse: [`visual-dna-lilibeth.md`](visual-dna-lilibeth.md).

### Philosophy

> **Find your brand voice.** Give AI the one thing it can't invent — taste.

**Voice before tone** — Decide what you stand for before you decide how you sound.

**Six decisions, locked once** — Voice · Colors · Type · Layout · Images · References. That's the whole brief.

**Show, don't tell** — The AI doesn't read your brand values. It pattern-matches your references.

**Restraint as a position** — Less is the only competitive advantage AI can't replicate by default.
